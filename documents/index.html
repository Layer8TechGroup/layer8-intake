Vertical-Specific Requirements

Some industries require additional documentation beyond the standard set. Select your vertical below to see what additional materials support the highest-confidence assessment in your sector.

Healthcare Healthcare Practices & Medical Groups
  • HIPAA Security Risk Assessment — most recent completed assessment
  • Provider licenses and DEA registrations — all providers, current copies
  • Payer contracts — top 5 by revenue; confirms transferability
  • Credentialing files — all clinical staff
  • Compliance training completion records — HIPAA and clinical compliance
  • Malpractice insurance certificates — all providers, current policy
Legal Law Firms & Legal Services Providers
  • Bar association standing confirmation — all attorneys of record
  • Malpractice insurance certificate — current policy declaration page
  • Client trust account reconciliation — most recent month
  • Matter management system export — active matter count and status
  • Conflicts check system documentation — demonstrates systematic risk management
Accounting / Financial Services CPA Firms, Bookkeeping & Financial Advisory
  • CPA license confirmation — all licensed CPAs at the firm
  • E&O insurance certificate — current policy
  • Engagement letter templates — demonstrates standardization and transferability
  • Client list with service type and recurring fee — by engagement type
  • Peer review report — most recent, if applicable
Insurance Insurance Agencies & Brokerages
  • Producer license documentation — all licensed staff, current state
  • E&O insurance certificate — current policy declaration page
  • Book of business summary — by line of business and carrier
  • Commission statement — most recent 12 months
  • Carrier appointment letters — all active carrier appointments
Technology / MSP Managed Service Providers & IT Services
  • PSA/RMM platform export — active client count and monthly recurring revenue
  • NOC documentation and escalation procedures — demonstrates systematized delivery
  • Client SLA agreements — sample showing contract terms and renewal structure
  • Vendor stack list with licensing details — all tools with contract terms
  • SOC 2 or security certification — if applicable
Real Estate Brokerages & Property Management
  • Brokerage license documentation — entity license, current
  • Agent roster with production by agent — demonstrates concentration / distribution
  • GCI breakdown by agent — last 12 months
  • Lead generation source attribution — owned vs. agent-dependent sources
  • Referral partner list and tracking — demonstrates systematized pipeline

How to Submit

A simple three-step process — no document upload required to begin.

1

Complete the Intake Form

Start at intake.layer8techgroup.com — company basics only, 5 minutes. No documents required to begin.

Complete Intake →
2

Receive Your Secure Upload Link

Within 24 hours of intake submission, Layer8 will send a secure document upload link. You control what you share and when.

3

Assessment Runs

Once documents are received, your scored report is delivered within 48–72 hours. The more documentation provided, the higher the scoring confidence.

About confidence levels: Every criterion in your report includes a confidence rating — High (3+ source documents corroborated), Moderate (2 documents), or Low (intake data only). Low-confidence scores should be reviewed before using in buyer negotiations. Providing supporting documents upgrades confidence ratings and produces more specific, defensible findings.
🔒

Privacy & Confidentiality

All documents submitted are treated as strictly confidential. Layer8 Tech Group does not share client materials with any third party without written consent. Documents are used solely to conduct the Exit Readiness Assessment and are not retained beyond the engagement without client authorization.
