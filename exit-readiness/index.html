Before You Start
What You'll Need

Company Basics

Legal name, industry vertical, years in operation, approximate employee count, and primary location.

e.g. "Meridian Pediatric Group, Healthcare, 12 years, 18 employees, Atlanta GA"

Financial Overview

Approximate annual revenue and EBITDA for the most recent full fiscal year. Exact figures are not required at intake.

e.g. "~$4.2M revenue, ~$1.1M EBITDA"

Exit Timeline

Your target window for going to market — this helps us prioritize which gaps matter most given your timeline.

e.g. "6–12 months", "12–24 months", "exploring options"

Primary Contact

Name, email, and phone for the owner or authorized representative. Assessment results are kept strictly confidential.

e.g. Owner, COO, or referring broker contact

💡 Don't have exact numbers? Estimates are fine at intake. The full assessment may request supporting documents (financial statements, contracts, org chart) after we've reviewed your intake — we'll guide you through that step.
